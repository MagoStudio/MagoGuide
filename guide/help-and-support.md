## Enterprise support

Enterprise customers receive:

- A dedicated support contact.
- Custom model training.
- Private cloud or on-premise deployment.
- NDA-grade IP terms.
- Territory-specific deployment options.

Contact through the **Pricing → Contact Us** flow for Enterprise inquiries.

## Debug Info panel

The **Debug Info** panel collects diagnostic information about the app, the current session, and the active render, to share with support when troubleshooting. Open it with **Ctrl + Shift + D**.

It exposes three copy actions:

- **Copy app info** — a summary of the app and environment state.
- **Copy section** — the currently expanded section only.
- **Copy all** — the entire debug report.

> **💡 Tip** — When reporting an issue, paste the copied debug information into the support channel. It speeds up diagnosis significantly.
